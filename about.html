About This Portfolio

This site is a personal lens on Zagreb — not the city of guidebooks, but the one I know from years of walking its streets. These photos are fragments of my everyday, captured with affection and honesty.

The images reflect textures, moods, and moments that shaped my experience. I hope they resonate with anyone who’s lived in a place long enough to see beyond its surface.
